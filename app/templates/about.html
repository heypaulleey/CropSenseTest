About CropSense

CropSense is an innovative soil health assessment platform developed by BSIT students of the University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines - Claveria Campus. It is designed exclusively for the Department of Agriculture - Claveria to support smarter, data-driven farming decisions.


Our system uses an ESP32-based handheld device equipped with real-time NPK, pH, moisture, and humidity sensors . Powered by machine learning-specifically a Random Forest model-CropSense analyzes soil health and provides accurate compatibility scores for maize, cabbage, and eggplant .


The project promotes digital transformation in agriculture by combining sensor technology, cloud-based data handling (Firebase), and a user-friendly web dashboard to empower local farmers and field technicians with timely crop recommendations.


We aim to make agriculture more efficient, sustainable, and accessible-starting right here in Claveria, Misamis Oriental .
